Capita.com Global Website Redesign

Led an enterprise-wide digital transformation for 45,000+ employees, redesigning the global website ecosystem to improve task completion by 60% and reduce support tickets by 40%.

Timeline
2022 – 2023
My Role
Lead UX Designer
Platform
Drupal CMS
Impact
45,000+ Employees

Context

The Challenge

Capita's corporate website had grown organically over years, resulting in inconsistent experiences, fragmented information architecture, and declining engagement metrics.

Key Problem Statement: How might we create a unified corporate digital experience that serves diverse user groups while establishing a scalable design system for future growth?

Discovery

Understanding the Problem Space

1

Stakeholder Workshops

Facilitated sessions with marketing, HR, investor relations, and IT.

2

Analytics Deep Dive

Analyzed 12 months of site data to identify drop-off points.

3

User Interviews

Conducted interviews with job seekers, investors, and existing clients.

4

Competitive Analysis

Evaluated 8 competitor corporate sites.

Key Findings

  • Navigation confusion: 45% of users abandoned before completing their task
  • Inconsistent branding: 5 different visual styles across sub-sites
  • Poor mobile experience: 38% of traffic was mobile, but the site wasn't fully responsive
  • Recruitment friction: 67% drop-off in job application process

Process

From Sketches to High-Fidelity

Hand Sketches & Responsive Planning

Early responsive sketches exploring layout patterns across breakpoints

Detailed Wireframing

Wireframes establishing content hierarchy, navigation structure, and component patterns

High-Fidelity Design

Final homepage design featuring hero video, service pillars, and content modules

Solution

Design System & Components

  • Modular page templates: 6 flexible templates that could create 50+ page variations
  • Workday integration: Seamless job search experience embedded within the site
  • Investor portal: Dedicated section with financial reports and governance info
  • Accessibility built-in: All components designed to WCAG 2.1 AA standards

Reflection

What I Learned

  • Sketch first, pixel later. The hand-sketching phase was invaluable for rapid stakeholder alignment.
  • Design systems pay compound returns. The upfront investment reduced ongoing design work by 50%.
  • Integration is everything. The Workday HRMS integration had the biggest impact on recruitment metrics.
