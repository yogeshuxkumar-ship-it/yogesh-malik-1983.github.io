Context

The Challenge

Zephyr Bank, a mid-sized regional bank with 850,000+ customers across the Midwest US, was losing market share to digital-first challenger banks. Their mobile app had a dismal 2.1-star rating, 68% uninstall rate within 30 days, and customers were actively avoiding digital channels.

Harman Connected Services was brought in to completely reimagine the mobile banking experience and help Zephyr compete in an increasingly digital-first financial landscape.

Key Problem Statement: How might we transform an underperforming mobile banking app into a trusted, intuitive experience that drives adoption and reduces branch dependency?

Reflection

What I Learned

  • Trust is everything in FinTech. Every interaction must reinforce security without creating friction. Biometric login was the single most impactful feature.
  • Test with real money anxieties. Users behave differently when actual finances are involved‚Äîlab testing didn't capture the stress of "is my money safe?"
  • Progressive disclosure beats tutorials. Users learn better by doing, not by reading. Our A/B tests proved this dramatically.
  • Accessibility expands your market. Features we added for accessibility (high contrast, larger touch targets) improved usability for everyone.
